== Description ==

Verelo watches your blog 24/7 to ensure that it is up and running without malware infections. Having your site go down, or expolited can damange your reputation and often result in hours of lost traffic. Using Verelo means you can detect issues far before your visitors notice. Verelo really just wants to make the Internet a better and more reliable place, <strong>so the service is completely free</strong>.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `verelo-monitoring.zip` onto your local computer.
2. Go to your WordPress Dashboard and select <strong>Plugins >> Add New</strong>.
3. Click on the <strong>Upload</strong> option at the top and select the `verelo-monitoring.zip file` you just downloaded.
4. Click on <strong>Install</strong>.
5. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
6. You should now have the "Verelo Blog Monitoring" menu on the left hand side of your dashboard, you will also find a login link here.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
- Can i disable Verelo? Yes, to do so login at http://www.verelo.com or email support@verelo.com with your email address & blog URL.
- Is Verelo blog monitoring free? Yes, until you want to monitor 2 or more sites or receive SMS or Phone notifications, you do not even need to enter a credit card.
- Do you watch my site for malware? Yes. We use the latest Malware and Virus detection technology, including the Google Safe Browsing API to ensure your site is Malware and Virus free.
- Is this a limited trial offer? No.
- How do i view my monitoring stats? Simply login to the Verelo dashboard using the information we emailed to the "Blog administrator email" address. You should get this email shortly after activating the plugin.
